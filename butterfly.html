M. Butterfly
David Henry Hwang’s play is a satirical critique of the orientalism in Puccini’s “Madama Butterfly.” A French diplomat travels to China and falls in love with an opera star who fulfills all of his fantasies of an Asian woman. But her calculated facade holds a secret: she is a spy, and a man! Using custom lettering, the poster and logotype’s sparkling ornamentation illustrate the performance of gender and cultural identity.

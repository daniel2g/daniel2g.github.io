Lock’n’Lube — Greasing Made Easy
Brochure exhibiting a prospective redesign of the Lock’n’Lube identity, created at Doug Lufkin Graphic Design. At this time, the company was in the process of changing the paint color of their flagship Grease Gun from red to yellow. As we could not wait to photograph the new product, our solution was to change the color in Photoshop, paying careful attention to the quality of the reflections.

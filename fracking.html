Fracking Ban Act
The Fracking Ban Act (S.3247) was introduced to the US Senate in January 2020 and dismissed by the majority. This poster uses the bill to illustrate the attitude of Congress toward environmental legislation: we see pages of Senator Sanders’ language torn up and discarded into a sea of solvent waste.
The stark photographic imagery frames the bill’s rejection as a crime scene prepared for the viewer’s investigation. Created using a combination of photography and 3d rendering.
